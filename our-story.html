Our Story

Oh yes! we have a story

  • Meenal Rai Shejwar Owner & Chef

    • Meenal Rai Shejwar, Owner and Pastry chef at Cake Tales is passionate about baking and is an avid lover of sugar art! Her inspiration for Baking came from her mother who used to bake for the kids at home. Since childhood Meenal has been baking for family and friends. She started gaining a lot of appreciation for her bakes. Meenal believes in the era of personalization when everything is being customized from pen to car then why not Bakes. Thus, she started up Cake Tales leaving behind her Chartered Accountancy! Cake Tales is a bhopal based start up where great taste and creative designs come together to make every occasion a memorable one! With their theory - "Theres a story behind every cake", Cake Tales has become quiet popular in town for their Custom designer Cakes, cupcakes, Dessert Tables, chocolates and hampers for all occasions.
